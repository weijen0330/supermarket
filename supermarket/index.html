Oranges of the week

Satsuma

Seedless and easy to peel, Satsumas are very sweet (they're known as "honey citrus" in China) with a tender, melt-in-your-mouth texture. They're great for eating out of hand.

Grapes of the week

Lakemont

These grapes maybe small, but they do pack a honey sweet, flavorful punch. One of the better tasting small grape varieties, such as Champagne grapes. These kid-sized grapes would make good editions to salads. They are just a fun variety that is worth checking out.

Avocados of the week

Hass

It's one of the fattiest and creamiest known varieties. Pick up some tomatoes and onions too to turn it into some delicious guacamole.

Blueberries of the week

Rabbiteye

Tend to be smaller, but still sweet and intensely flavorful. Rabbiteye berries are loaded with Antioxidant and Vitamin C.

Mangoes of the week

Ataulfos

Imported from Mexico, Ataulfos have a very small seed, so there is a high flesh to seed ratio. It has smooth, firm flesh with no fibers.
